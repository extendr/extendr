where E: ComplexField,

Solves the equation $\text{Op}(A) x = \text{rhs}$ in the sense of least squares, where $\text{Op}$ is either the identity or the conjugate, depending on the value of conj, and stores the result in the upper part of rhs.

work is a temporary workspace with the same dimensions as rhs.
